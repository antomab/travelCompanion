Welcome to the Travel Companion test!

We'll guide you through a quick on-boarding process, followed by two short scenarios.

Scenario #1 - At the bus stop

You've just arrived at the bus stop and the assistant informs you of the buses you can take in order to get to your destination.

With the gestures you just learned, try to find out where each bus is bound to, and select one when you're ready to "board".

Scenario #1 Complete!

We have a few questions about the task you just completed. It should only take 1 or 2 minutes. To start the survey use your phone assistant and say:

"Alexa/Google, Launch SurveyLine."

And when prompted, say the name of the survey:

"Travel Companion One"

When you're ready, move on to the next scenario.

Scenario #2 - Following a Walking Guide

You are walking around a piazza in Rome, following your walking guide tour. Explore your surroundings and try to learn more about the buildings in front of you.

When you're finished, exit the walking guide by calling up the menu and selecting "Exit Navigation".

.

Scenario #2 Complete!

You have completed the Travel Companion test. Before you leave, we have a few more questions about the test. To start the survey use your phone assistant and say:

"Alexa/Google, Launch SurveyLine."

And when prompted, say the name of the survey:

"Travel Companion Two"

Thank you for taking part in this test!
